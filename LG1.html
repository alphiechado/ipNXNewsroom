Changes in IS&T

Dear All,
Hope we have had a good weekend. Thank God for the gift of a new week.
It is with mixed feelings that we announce the exit of the indefatigable Daniel Okodaso, Head of IS&T department from ipNX with effect from Wednesday 26th, 2022. Daniel is departing the shores of this country to begin another phase of life with his newly wedded wife. Whilst we wish he could stay to continue to implement the IS&T Change Plan, we cannot but wish him the best in his future endeavours.


Daniel and the rest of his capable and dynamic team have been working closely with Executive Management to achieve a smooth transition and are handling it with remarkable professionalism, commitment and I dare say, courage. To that end, Management hereby announces that Femi Ayilara, Head of Applications Development in IS&T, will become the Acting Head of the IS&T Department, reporting to the Group ED, HR & CS; also with effect from Wednesday 26th, 2022. The IS&T team is fully on board to ensure they continue to demonstrate the level of service that is expected of them, and Executive Management is confident that they will deliver on their mandate.
We ask for your usual support, patience and collaboration as Femi and the Team will continue to reposition and settle down to deliver.
BIDA The BIDA unit has been moved to IS&T. Frances Nnamadim, Acting Head of BIDA is hereby confirmed as Head of BIDA reporting to the Head of IS&T.
It is easy to locate Daniel, Femi and Frances on our riveting Zoho workplace and extend your best regards to them. Different greetings for each of them.
Thank you all for your anticipated cooperation.


Kind Regards,


Folashade Efiong-Bassey
Group Executive Director, HR & Corporate Services
ipNX Nigeria Limited
4, Balarabe Musa Crescent
Victoria Island, Lagos
