Meritorious Retirement - Rita Okoji-Adesoji

Dear Colleagues,
In line with the Company’s retirement policy, we are pleased to announce the meritorious retirement of Mrs. Rita Okoji-Adesoji, who was the Head of  the Supply Chain Management, in the Corporate Center.
Rita was a founding member of the team that was deployed from Telnet Nigeria Limited to ipNX, then named Netco.
She has served in various capacities for over 25 years,  both in the Supply Chain Management Department and as Head of CEA,  during which her contributions brought stability and transformation to the teams
she led in general, and the organization at large.

Just a very few of her many and notable milestones at ipNX include:
Set up of the Supply Chain Management function
The design and  implementation  the PARIC application (the enterprise procurement application) and being in the forefront of process automation at ipNX.
Completion of the Customer Self Service Portal (CSSP)
Successfully managed services to FOS customers through the introduction of DATA CAP
A mentor to many leaders in the organization
While applauding her commitment and dedication, we commend her leadership, astute negotiation skills and invaluable service rendered to the organization.
Although retired ( and definitely not tired!) she is still around with us for a while on a Post Retirement Contract, working with the Group Executive Director, Commercials.
Please join us to congratulate her on  the achievement of this hallmark in her career and wish her the best in the next steps of her journey.
Rita can be reached on rokoji-adesoji@ipnxnigeria.net for any goodwill messages.
 
Kind Regards,
 
Folashade Efiong-Bassey
Group Executive Director, HR & Corporate Services
ipNX Nigeria Limited
4, Balarabe Musa Crescent
Victoria Island, Lagos
