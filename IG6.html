Meritorious Retirement - Lawal Adeshina

Dear Colleagues,
In line with the Company’s retirement policy, we are pleased to announce the meritorious retirement of Mr. Lawal Adeshina, the Head,  Special Projects (Tax and Regulatory / Fixed Assets), Finance Department of the Corporate Centre. Lawal is a seasoned  Chartered accountant (ICAN). Since January 2010, Lawal has contributed his immense expertise to the development and stability of the reporting, compliance and regulatory  function of the Finance Team. Perhaps not everyone knows of his multilingual ability and mellifluous dexterity in speaking the sonorous Hausa language. He, however is well known for his calm and urbane demeanour, steady interpersonal skills and listening ear. Some of the notable achievements of Lawal include:
The development of a streamlined database for financial reporting.
The reconciliation process for updated financial record management and seamless financial reporting.
Participated in the implementation of the current version of SunSystems which aided the migration to a web- based financial application.
Successful management of finance related regulatory relationships on behalf of the company, to mitigate attendant risks.
We commend his commitment, integrity, loyalty, and dedicated service to the organization.
Although retired, ( of course, not tired! ) he is still around with us for a while on a Post Retirement Contract working with the CFO, George Aremu on  finance operations and reporting.
Please join us to congratulate Lawal on the achievement of this milestone in his career and wish him the very best in the next steps of his journey.
Lawal can be reached on ladeshina@ipnxnigeria.net  for goodwill messages.
Kind Regards,
Folashade Efiong-Bassey
Group Executive Director, HR & Corporate Services
ipNX Nigeria Limited
4, Balarabe Musa Crescent
Victoria Island, Lagos
