Organizational Changes - Infrastructure and Retail

Dear All,
As part of continued organizational repositioning for increased efficiency, attention to optimal resourcing and appointing leadership from within, Group Management is pleased to announce the following changes:

1. Chris Oputteh formerly Head of Engineering Retail Division now becomes the Deputy Divisional Chief Executive Officer (DDCEO), Operations, Infrastructure Division.
2. Uche Nnakenyi is now the DDCEO, IPQ and Projects, Infrastructure Division.
2. Emmanuel Yusuf formerly Lead, Operations and Maintenance of Retail Division becomes the Ag Head of Engineering, Retail Division.

The changes take effect April 11th, 2023.

We trust that our colleagues can continue to count on our usual maximum cooperation and support as they take on these new challenges.

Congratulations Emmanuel! We wish you good success and maximum achievement.
Congratulations to Chris too as he crosses into DDCEO leadership!
