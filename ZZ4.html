TECH BOOST (WORKPLACE QUICK TIPS)

Are you planning your annual leave, or will you be out of the office and don't want to leave a co-worker or client stranded in your absence?
Do you know this is simplified with the Zoho Mail Out of Office feature?
Making a "out of office" auto-reply email is one of the most crucial things to do before leaving the office. You must let your clients, co-workers, and superiors know how and when they can get in touch with you again. Even if you only intend to stay home and relax, you should still do that. It exhibits a particular degree of professionalism.
You can alert people of your whereabouts and instruct them on what to do while you're away with an out-of-office message. Your out-of-office message includes options for critical concerns as well.
These emails are essential, especially if you have established relationships with clients who require rapid service.
